10 Iconic David Bowie Career Moments

After losing his battle to liver cancer on January 10, 2016, the Starman returned to his place amid the cosmos, leaving in his comet's trail wake a universally celebrated career bursting with countless iconic moments, from culture defining-albums to legendary sartorial statements to beloved cult film roles.

A true visionary in every sense of the word, the glam rock legend helped pioneer the shape of modern artistry and entertainment. A musician, a poet, a performer, a pop star, an actor, a showman, a fashion trendsetter: Bowie was all of the above, paving the way for future multi-talented artists like Madonna, Michael Jackson and Lady Gaga.
