Utilizing Smudge Sticks in Phasmophobia: A Comprehensive Guide

The Importance of Smudge Sticks in Phasmophobia

Phasmophobia, a popular horror game, challenges players to confront paranormal entities in eerie environments. Smudge Sticks are essential tools that can help players survive these terrifying encounters. While optional, these items are invaluable as they provide a crucial defense mechanism against malevolent spirits. In the game, losing items due to player death not only impacts survival but also diminishes rewards.

When ignited, Smudge Sticks create a safety barrier, preventing ghosts from entering their hunting phase for a limited time. By purchasing them from the in-game Item Store for $15, players gain a powerful advantage in countering supernatural threats.

Activation of Smudge Sticks can be done in two ways – either by using a lighter to ignite them, creating a protective shield, or by interacting with them while holding a lit candle or lighter. These actions are crucial, especially in high-difficulty hunts, making Smudge Sticks a necessity for mastering the challenges of Phasmophobia and ensuring player survival.

Instructions for Using Smudge Sticks in Phasmophobia

Here is a step-by-step guide on effectively using Smudge Sticks in Phasmophobia:

  • Access the Item Store in Phasmophobia’s main menu.
  • Navigate to the Optional Equipment category.
  • Purchase Smudge Sticks for $15 of in-game currency.
  • Ensure you have Smudge Sticks in your mission inventory.

Equipping Lighter or Candle:

To use Smudge Sticks, ensure you have either a lighter or a lit candle in your inventory. Follow these steps:

  • If you have a lighter, hold the Smudge Sticks and activate them (default key: F) to initiate a 6-second burn and activate their protective effects.
  • Alternatively, interact with Smudge Sticks while holding a lit candle or lighter to ignite them, creating a protective smoke.

Smudge Sticks serve two main purposes: preventing ghosts from entering their hunting phase for 90 seconds within a 6-meter radius and halting a ghost’s hunting for 6 seconds, allowing for an escape. Using Smudge Sticks at critical moments is crucial due to their fast-burning nature, especially in high-difficulty hunts.

Reasons to Acquire Smudge Sticks

Obtaining Smudge Sticks in Phasmophobia significantly increases a player’s chances of surviving ghost encounters. These items, though optional, serve as a vital defense against vengeful spirits, mitigating the risks of death and item loss in the game. Smudge Sticks provide a temporary shield against ghostly attacks, offering a window of safety crucial for exploration and survival in Phasmophobia’s perilous world.

Lighting Smudge Sticks in Phasmophobia

Mastering the art of lighting Smudge Sticks is essential for ghost hunters in Phasmophobia. Follow these steps:

  • Ensure you have a lighter in your inventory, as it’s necessary for activating Smudge Sticks.
  • Equip the Smudge Stick and use the “Use” key (typically bound to ‘F’) to ignite it with the flame from your lighter.
  • As the Smudge Stick emits smoke, it actively wards off ghosts, safeguarding you from potential attacks and preserving your sanity.
  • Use Smudge Sticks judiciously, especially in tense situations or high-difficulty hunts, where they can make a difference between survival and ghostly demise.

Overview of Phasmophobia

Phasmophobia, developed and published by Kinetic Games, is a paranormal horror game inspired by ghost hunting. Initially released as an early access title on Steam for Windows with virtual reality support, it gained immense popularity among gamers and streamers, becoming a global bestseller on Steam during its launch period.

Its success can be attributed to its immersive gameplay, challenging players to navigate haunted environments and confront supernatural entities. The allure of Phasmophobia lies in its thrilling ghost investigations, making it a captivating experience for horror enthusiasts worldwide.
