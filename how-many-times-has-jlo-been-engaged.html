How many times has JLo been engaged?

JENNIFER Lopez and Alex Rodriguez have reportedly called off their two-year engagement.

The couple broke up following cheating speculation from the MLB star with Southern Charm’s Madison LeCroy.

How many times has JLo been engaged?

After reportedly breaking up with the MLB star, Lopez has been engaged a total of three times.

The 51-year-old met Rodriguez 20 years ago when the baseball star asked for the popstar’s autograph and again at New York Yankees game.

It was first announced the couple was dating in March 2017.

Who are JLo’s other ex’s?

Ojani Noa

Lopez was briefly married to Cuban waiter Ojani Noa.

The pair wed on February 22, 1997, but split less than a year later.

Since then, Lopez has endured legal challenges regarding her relationship with Noa, including in 2006 when she sued to prevent him publishing a book about their marriage.

Sean Combs

While working on her debut album On The 6, Lopez began dating record producer and rapper Sean Combs.

In December 1999, the pair were arrested along with two others following a shooting outside a club in Times Square.

Lopez was quickly exonerated and had nothing to do with the incdient.

Combs was charged with four weapons-related charges, but was found not guilty.

Cris Judd

After splitting with Combs, Lopez began to date former back-up dancer Cris Judd.

They wed on September 29, 2001.

But this too was a brief marriage, as they divorced less than a year later in June 2002, citing  irreconcilable differences.

Ben Affleck

Just months after divorcing Judd,  Lopez became engaged to actor and filmmaker Ben Affleck in November 2002.

They were dubbed “Bennifer” and quickly became a prominent supercouple.

The pair were due to get married in September 2003, but postponed the wedding indefinitely just a day before the ceremony.

Lopez and Affleck then split in January 2004.

Marc Anthony

Following her heartbreak with Ben Affleck, Lopez started a relationship with her longtime friend, singer Marc Anthony.

She married Anthony in June 2004, and on November 7, 2007, during the final night of their co-headlining tour, they couple announced they were expecting their first child.

Lopez gave birth to twins Emme and Maxilian in February 2008.

But three years later, in July 2011, the couple announced their split and filed for divorce in April 2012.

Their divorce was finalised in 2014, with Lopez retaining primary custody of the twins.
