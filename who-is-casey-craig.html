Casey Craig’s Background and Career

Casey Craig is not just your average reality TV star. Before joining the ‘Winter House’ cast, she made a name for herself in the world of cryptocurrency. As the Global Head of Communications at CoinDesk, Casey is a respected professional in the field. Her expertise and knowledge in this rapidly evolving industry have undoubtedly contributed to her success.

Casey Craig’s Appearance on ‘Winter House’


In the upcoming season of ‘Winter House,’ Casey makes a dramatic entrance that sets her apart from the rest of the cast. Casey Craig’s Other Experiences

Beyond her career in technology and reality TV, Casey Craig has had a diverse range of experiences. Before diving into the world of cryptocurrency, she was a professional skier, competing in the US back in 2009. Additionally, Casey has also worked as a ski instructor and even dabbled in modeling. Her varied background has undoubtedly shaped her into the dynamic and multifaceted individual she is today.

Casey Craig’s Family and Personal Life


Casey Craig comes from a well-established family. Her parents, Tommy and Kim Craig, reside in Fairfield, Connecticut, and have been happily married for over three decades. Tommy, a former senior vice president at Hines Interests, recently retired after an impressive 40-year career. Casey’s upbringing in a supportive and successful family environment has likely played a role in shaping her own ambitions and drive.

Casey Craig’s Relationship with Christopher Heyn

Casey Craig is currently in a relationship with Christopher Heyn, the founder of Havoc Henry, a fashion brand and creative agency. Their relationship became public in April 2021, and Casey has shared glimpses of their life together on social media. Christopher’s company draws inspiration from various sources, blending elements of Old Hollywood glamour, surf culture, and streetwear. Their shared interests and mutual support make them a dynamic couple.
