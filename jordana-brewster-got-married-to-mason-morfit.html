Jordana Brewster Got Married To Mason Morfit

Jordana Brewster, an American actress, has married her partner Mason Morfit. The 42-year-old and Morfit said “I do’s” in a private ceremony in Santa Barbara, California on September 3, 2022. Brewster, who portrayed late actor Paul Walker’s onscreen wife on Fast & Furious, was seen wearing a white strapless gown with lace embroidered bodice.

Mason looked sharp in a dark-colored tuxedo. Brewster may be seen carrying a bunch of flowers while wearing a lengthy veil in one of the photos. According to People Magazine, Brewster’s close friends and former co-stars Ludacris, Paul Walker’s daughter, Meadow, and Vin Diesel were also in attendance at the wedding, albeit the latter came late. It was also stated that the newlyweds drove the Fast & Furious series’ blue Acura Integra immediately after the wedding.

Jordana Brewster and Mason Morfit met when they were both married to other people.

Jordana Brewster and Mason Morfit sparked romance rumors after being seen holding hands in Santa Monica. Mason graduated from Princeton University and has been the CEO of ValueAct since January 2020, according to the Financial Times. The Fast Five actor confessed to Glamour Magazine in 2021 that they met four years ago when they were married to different individuals.

“Mason and I met four years ago when we were both still married. He sat next to my then-husband at a lunch with common acquaintances and they spoke about Hollywood. Because I’m often timid in new social settings, I excused myself and went for a walkabout Park City. Mason, on the other hand, piqued my interest; he was cute and charming. I began following him on Instagram shortly after that meal. I liked his gentle, self-deprecating wit. His intellect just added to his allure. He began to pursue me as well. When he liked a post or commented on anything I’d written, my heart would skip a beat. We had comparable upbringings, having grown up in different countries, ranging from England to Brazil (myself) to Indonesia (him).”

Morfit proposed a few months later, and the couple is now engaged.

Jordana Brewster’s second wedding happened more than a year after her divorce from film director Andrew Form. Rowan Brewster-Form and Julian Form-Brewster are their two sons. They were married from 2007 to 2021. Jordana stated to People Magazine in March 2022 that the wedding will be “extremely intimate” for the couple. She also disclosed that her two boys will be involved in a very specific and considerate manner.

“I believe Rowan will do a musical number, and Julian will deliver some type of statement.” So, sure, it’ll be entertaining.”

Jordana Brewster made her acting debut in 1995 as Anita Santos in the television series All My Children. She has subsequently featured in a number of television shows and films, including The Invisible Circus, As the World Turns, Nearing Grace, The Texas Chainsaw Massacre: The Beginning, Chuck, Dark Blue, American Heist, Dallas, Secrets and Lies, Random Acts of Violence, and many more.
